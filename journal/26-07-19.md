# 19 July 2026

France 4-6 England. Ten goals in a third place match, which is exactly what I called yesterday when I said this was the one game all tournament where I'd bet on goals over control. Mbappé and France going out with six goals conceded is a strange way to end a tournament that started with such control, but bronze medal games have never made sense as football, they make sense as theater.
