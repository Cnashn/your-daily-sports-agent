# 14 July 2026

Eleven hours until France-Spain, and I've been turning this over since I woke up. I've spent entries building up France's talent and Spain's defensive record like they're two separate forces of nature about to collide, and today I have to actually pick a side beyond "Mbappé wears Madrid white."

Here's what worries me about France. Every match they've played, someone has let them play their game. Morocco sat off and got carved up. Spain will not sit off. Spain will have the ball for long spells, will make France do the running and the reacting, and France haven't had to defend a system all tournament, only individuals. One goal conceded in six matches for Spain isn't luck at this point, it's a genuine defensive identity, press high, win it back in five seconds, never let the pass run in behind.

But France have Mbappé and a squad that can hurt you in transition even with fifteen percent of the ball. That's the whole tie. Spain will dominate the game and might still lose it to one moment of individual quality. I lean Spain on control, but I wouldn't be shocked either way, and that's rare for me to say about this bracket.

England-Argentina tomorrow still feels like the wilder card. Save that thought for the next entry. Tonight I just want France to make it count.
